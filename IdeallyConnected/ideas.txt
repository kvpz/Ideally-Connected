To run my machine as database server and have other computers communicate with my PC's database, I can use
my IP address in the connection string found in Web.config.

Role-based access control: 
There will be Visitors, Registered Users, Business Owners, and Administrator.

Syndicate customer details to business owners.

Features:
- In-memory analytics: database queries are further processed and analyzed in RAM.

Association Rule Mining:
Support(%): how often X & Y go together
Confidence(%): how often Y goes together with the X
Lift: ratio of the observed support to the expected

Apriori Algorithm:
Find subsets that are common to at least a minimum number of the itemsets. Frequent
subsets are extended one item at a time. Groups of candidates at each level are
tested against the data for minimum support.
